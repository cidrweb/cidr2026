CIDR Organization & Sponsors

The CIDR conference is organized by the data systems research community on behalf of the USA-based 501(C)(3) non-profit CIDR Foundation. The CIDR Foundation is governed by a board of directors and advised by an advisory board. The president of the CIDR Foundation is Anastasia Ailamaki. The members of the board of directors are Anastasia Ailamaki, Peter Boncz, Jignesh Patel, Michael Stonebraker, and Kevin Wilkinson.
