CIDR 2026 Registration

Venue

CIDR 2026 will be held from Sunday, January 18, 2026, to Wednesday, January 21, 2026 at Chaminade Resort & Spa, a mountaintop hotel on 300 acres in Santa Cruz, California. Chaminade is a 75 minute drive from San Francisco airport and just 45 minutes from San Jose airport. Chaminade features modern, spacious guest rooms in eleven villas nestled among eucalyptus and redwood forests. The resort includes a fitness center, pool, hot tub, spa services, hiking trails and tennis. The conference center has sweeping views of Monterey Bay and the Santa Cruz mountains. It is a relatively small resort so CIDR will occupy most, if not all, of the space.

Registration

Registration is a two-step process.

Conference registration

First, register to CIDR and pay the conference fee at EventBrite.

Accommodation

Second, reserve your room at Chaminade. The room fee includes meals.

Single rooms: should be booked through the website Chaminade CIDR 2026 Conference. The room rate of $1357.35 includes all taxes and fees.

Shared Rooms: cannot be booked through the website. You can book a shared room by emailing sales@Chaminade.com. Please identify your roommate before contacting Chaminade. The shared room rate is $929.94.

Attendees who wish to arrive early or stay after the concert should contact sales@Chaminade.com. The daily rate is $220 plus taxes and fees.

Note:: The Chaminade website may show a total charge higher than either $1357.35 or $929.94. This is a problem with their website. You will be charged the contracted rate listed above.

Contact

Our point of contact at Chaminade is Krysta Neff. You may call the sales team at (831) 465-3421, but email is preferred.

Attendees may share a room with friends or family who are not attendeding CIDR. They may use all the facilities at Chaminade but they should add their names to the reservation at check-in or by emailing sales.

Cancellation

Registration may be cancelled with a full refund up to 30 days before the start of CIDR.

If you have questions, email CIDR local arrangements: info@cidrdb.org.
